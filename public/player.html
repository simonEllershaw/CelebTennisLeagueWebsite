Rules

  • Each tennis match is the first to one set
  • Must win by 2 clear games or at 6-6 a tie break is played
  • 3 points are awarded for a win, 0 for a lose
  • If points are tied game difference then games won and finaly games lost determine standings
  • Every player plays every other player twice, once at home and once away
  • Games occur on a weekly basis
